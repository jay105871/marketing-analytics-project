# 📊 Marketing Analytics & Customer Segmentation Project

## 🔍 Overview
This project analyzes customer behavior data to identify meaningful customer segments using Python and KMeans clustering. The goal is to improve marketing targeting and business decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Businesses need to understand customer behavior to improve marketing efficiency and personalization. This project groups customers into actionable segments based on income and spending behavior.

---

## 🧰 Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Scikit-learn (KMeans Clustering)
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## ⚙️ Workflow
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Feature engineering (Income, Total Spend, Age, Purchases)  
- Exploratory data analysis  
- KMeans clustering for segmentation  
- Visualization of results  

---

## 🤖 Customer Segments
- High Value Customers  
- High Income / Low Spend  
- Low Income / High Spend  
- Low Value Customers  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- A small group of customers generates most revenue  
- Spending behavior is not directly tied to income  
- Clear segmentation enables targeted marketing strategies  

---

## 💼 Business Impact
- Improves customer targeting  
- Supports personalized marketing campaigns  
- Helps optimize marketing spend  
- Enables data-driven decision-making
